Audio-Driven Lip Sync User Study

Comparing lip-sync video models

What you'll do

You'll see 10 sets of 4 short videos. Each set shows the same person:

  • The leftmost video is the Ground Truth (real recording).
  • The other three are different lip-sync model outputs, shown without labels.

For each set, watch the four videos (they loop, and the scrubber on any one moves all four together), then rate the three unlabeled videos on four dimensions.

Questions you'll be asked (per video)

  1. Sync — How well is the video synced with the audio?
  2. Video Quality — How is the visual quality of the video?
  3. ID Preservation — How well does the video preserve the identity of the person across time?
  4. Naturalness — How natural does the video look?

Each on a 1-5 scale (1 = poor, 5 = excellent). The Ground Truth video does not need to be rated.

Practical notes

  • Headphones recommended.
  • Allow ~10–15 minutes.
  • Your progress is saved locally as you go — if your browser closes mid-study, you can resume from the same place.
  • At the end, your responses are submitted automatically. No personal information is collected beyond the demographic dropdowns.
By starting, you agree your anonymized responses will be used for academic research.
